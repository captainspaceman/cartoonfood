INFOGRAPGHIC MARKETING - BRICKHOUSE

The Job:
Brickhouse Security wanted to strategically boost search engine rankings while simultaneously putting out interesting content. No small task for a security company.

What we did:

It had occurred to me that people tweet more about pop culture than they do about important cultural events. We had access to a tool that could count tweets by context and phrases used within those tweets. It was called Topsy. We then did a side by side comparison, collected the information into a graphic and voila.

Results
300,000+ views and impressions across 50+ organic cross-publications
Overall 25% lift in organic keyword performance as a result of inbound link growth
14,000 Facebook shares and 1,000 Twitter tweets
Cross publication in Adweek, the Huffington Post and Social Media Today
